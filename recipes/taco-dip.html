Taco Dip

Description

Don't let the simplicity of this recipe fool you. If you're in the need for a fast, easy dip to make for your next party, the simpler, the better. Trust Me!

Ingredients

  • One Stick of Real Butter
  • One pound ground beef (85% lean or higher)
  • One 16oz. Bag of Shredded Cheese (Mexican Blend or your preferred flavor)
  • Two 8oz. Cans of Black Beans (drained)
  • One 8oz. jar of your favorite salsa

Directions

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet and drain some or all of the grease. Some may prefer to keep a little for extra flavor
  2. Season beef to taste, cover, and let simmer while continuing on to the next steps
  3. In a large saucepan melt the entire stick of butter
  4. Once butter is liquified, add the two cans of black beans
  5. Salt beans and butter to taste, stir and bring to a simmer for a few minutes
  6. Add the cooked and seasoned ground beef, stir and bring to a simmer once again
  7. Add the entire bag of cheese and stir continuously until the cheese is melted completely
  8. Add the entire jar of salsa and stir until blended evenly
  9. Pour taco dip into a dish and serve with your favorite chips
  10. For best results use a warming dish or crockpot to keep the dip melted
