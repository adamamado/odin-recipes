Cheese Pizza

Description

It's pretty difficult to mess up a cheese pizza. It's just dough, sauce and cheese, right? But if you're looking to get a bit more milage from your cheese pizz, then I suggest you give this recipe a go.

Ingredients

  • One Pre-made Pizza Crust (I prefer Boboli Crusts)
  • One jar/can of your favorite pizza sauce (Here's my favorite sauce)
  • One 8oz bag of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • One 8oz bag of shredded cheddar cheese
  • One 4oz container of crumbled feta cheese(This is the real secret!)

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to the recommended temperature on the pizza crust package
  2. Remove one pre-made curst from package and place on pizza pan
  3. Open the pizza sauce and distribute sauce evenly over the surface of the crust. Use your preferred amount of sauce.
  4. Apply a thin layer of mozzarella cheese on top of sauce
  5. Apply a thin layer of cheddar cheese on top of mozzarella
  6. Make sure the feta cheese is adequately crumbled. Use a fork to break up large chunks
  7. Sprinkle a moderate amount of feta crumbles on top of your pizza
  8. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes or until crust is golden brown
  9. Let pizza cool for at least 5 minutes
  10. Slice and serve!
